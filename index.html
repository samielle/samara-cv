Summary

My passion is creating meaningful experiences and human-centred design solutions. As a senior creative leading the UX/UI product design for a sustainability startup, I currently enjoy developing intelligent solutions for users with the added perk of creating positive change for humanity and our planet. My 17 years of experience in the creative industry includes travelling the world as a professional photographer and designing a digital tide watch range for the global surf brand, Billabong. I love exploring and understanding human experiences, challenges and psychology to build projects that inclusively enhance people's digital experiences. As a full-stack designer with a holistic mindset from big-picture ideation to debating the pixel-level nuances of interactions on a button, I am open-minded, curious about new ways to solve problems and enjoy being multi-disciplinary while embracing and welcoming change. I look forward to sharing my experience and continuing to flex, grow and collaborate with others as a senior product designer, exploring new innovative ways of problem-solving various projects we develop.
